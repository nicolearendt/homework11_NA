Max Temperature

As expected, the weather is signicantly warmer the closer you are to the equator (0 Degrees Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer than the northern hemisphere this time of year. This may be because of the tilt of the earth.
